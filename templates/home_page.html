Welcome to आकार - Shaping your finances

Say goodbye to broke mornings and wondering why you’re surviving on instant noodles. Aakar is the smart, hassle-free finance buddy designed for college students who want to get their money game on point—without feeling like they’re attending a finance class.

What’s Aakar All About?

We’re here to help you master your finances in a way that feels as easy as scrolling through your socials. Whether it’s tracking how much you spent on coffee this month or figuring out why your wallet feels lighter, we’ve got your back!

Here’s How We Keep You Winning:

- All About You: Create your profile and start tracking your cash flow. You’re in control—add, update, or delete transactions anytime, no judgment here.

- Budget Like a Boss: Set your monthly budget (we promise it’s super easy). As you spend, we keep an eye on your expenses. If you go overboard, we’ll show you exactly where things went wild with clear, no-nonsense graphs.

- Track Your Money Vibes: Explore trends in your spending habits through colorful, interactive charts. See how much you’re spending and where—whether it’s on food, fun, or “I couldn’t resist” purchases.

- Be Financially Woke: Get a full view of your spending patterns and learn how to save for the things that really matter—like that weekend trip or upgrading your gadgets.

Aakar isn’t just an app; it’s your go-to tool for taking charge of your money while keeping life fun. Adulting may seem tough, but managing your finances doesn’t have to be.

Ready to shape your finances and level up? Join the Aakar fam today and show your wallet who’s boss!
